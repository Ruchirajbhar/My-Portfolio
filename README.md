# Ruchi Rajbhar — Compliance Portfolio

I'm a law graduate (B.A. LL.B. Hons.) transitioning into AML/KYC compliance. My background is in litigation and legal research, and over the past year, I've been building domain knowledge in financial regulation, studying PMLA, RBI KYC Master Directions, FATF frameworks, and completing an AML/KYC certification.

This portfolio is a case study I built to demonstrate that understanding practically. All four documents follow the same fictional customer, Rajiv Mehta, an import-export business owner, through the full compliance lifecycle.

---

## The Case Study

**Rajiv Mehta** — Individual business owner, textile machinery importer with cross-border remittances to UAE and Singapore. Medium-High risk profile. The documents follow him in sequence:

**01 — Compliance Risk Assessment**
Entity-level risk evaluation covering AML/KYC gaps, TBML exposure, third-party risk, and transaction monitoring weaknesses — with specific mitigation controls.
→ [View Report](./Compliance_Risk_Assessment_Report.pdf)

**02 — Customer Due Diligence (CDD)**
Full customer due diligence. UBO identification, risk factor analysis, EDD assessment, STR filing decision.
→ [View Report](./Customer_Due_Diligence_CDD.pdf)

**03 — KYC Report**
Customer identification and verification. Document review, PEP and sanctions screening, false positive documentation, preliminary risk assessment.
→ [View Report](./KYC_Report.pdf)

**04 — Suspicious Activity Report (SAR)**
Structured cash deposits flagged across April–June 2025. Smurfing pattern identified, grounds for suspicion documented, FIU-IND filing recommended under PMLA Section 12.
→ [View Report](./Suspicious_Activity_Report_SAR.pdf)
